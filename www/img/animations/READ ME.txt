PIXEL ANIMATIONS PACK

Thank you for downloading this graphics package.


Included:
 - Animation Sheets (100%)
 - Animation Sheets for RMVX/RMMV
 - Individual Frames

Most animations are designed to be flexible. Some elements can be used in combinations with other animations.
Some animations can loop infinitely -- hopefully it's clear which ones are capable of this.
